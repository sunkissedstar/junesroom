Henford-on-Bagley

This is about my Sims I love very dearly; Yvonne Grey and Raelyn Witt. Explaning my ocs are difficult because what I might know for one character, I couldn't have a slightest clue for the other. And by no means am I a writter. This is here for me to share and enjoy.
Henford, in my mind, is a relatively small county. Everyone knows each other. And it's a Hispanic / Black majority. Western vibes, great soil, you’d often see more wild horses than people when out in public. There are always new homes being built, and it helps the community grow stronger, as well as giving old retired men something to do.

Yvonne Grey

Yvonne, American born in Turkey, fully white. When her mother died, at ten, she was sent to be raised by her single father, Bill Grey, in Henford, U.S. She has wavy auburn-brown hair, styled in a choppy, ear-length cut. She was mid-sized until her early 20s, when she began her weight loss journey. She later found that she couldn't lose weight in her legs due to lipedema. (She also suffers from bisexuality)

Raelyn Witt

Raelyn, born in Florida, U.S., Mexican and Indigenous. She and her twin brother were both raised by both of their parents on a generational ranch in Henford-on-Bagley. She has an eye condition, and is predicted to be a little more than partially blind by 45. She has wispy jet black hair and dark brown eyes. Dark femme type. Lesbian
